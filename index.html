How to Break The Youtube
Open Google Chrome or Firefox and drag-and-drop this link to your bookmark bar:

Break The YouTube

Then, go to a YouTube music video, like this one for example, and click on the bookmark after the YouTube page has fully loaded.

Chrome+macOS users
If you can't drag-and-drop the link:

  1. Right-click the link and click "Copy Link Address"
  2. Bookmark this page and click "Edit...""
  3. Clear the URL field and paste the link you just copied instead.

If it doesn't work:
This project was only tested with Google Chrome and Firefox, with the current YouTube version served to my computer.
If it doesn't seem to work with your setup, the alternative is to watch this video.

Optional keyboard controls
Q: toggle view count
W: toggle bottom section
E: toggle right section
A: toggle top section
S: toggle triangles
