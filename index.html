Drag and drop this link to your bookmark bar :

Break The YouTube

and click on it while playing AC/DC - T.N.T. (from Live at River Plate) on YouTube.
With other videos, you'll need to enable the effects by pressing the QWERTZ keys.


Other controls :
A/D : +/- adjust the beat detection threshold.
S : disable/enable beat detection.
U : skip the end of the scenario for TNT.


Notes :
Only tested with Google Chrome and the current YouTube version served to my computer.
It might not work on all YouTube versions.
